Our mission

We were created in early 2025 in order to combine and unite all Azerbaijani students among all the campuses of SJTU. We aim to build a reliable peer network for academic and everyday support, help new students integrate smoothly, and create a strong sense of community. In parallel, we promote Azerbaijani culture through respectful representation and participation in university-wide activities.

Community support

Practical help, peer guidance, and a welcoming environment for new and current students.

Cultural representation

Participation in festivals and events that bring Azerbaijani culture to the SJTU community.
