Who We Are

Our Mission

We were created in early 2025 to connect and unite Azerbaijani students across all campuses of Shanghai Jiao Tong University. Our goal is to build a welcoming community where students support one another, share practical guidance, and feel at home in Shanghai. Through events and cultural representation, we share Azerbaijani culture with the wider SJTU community in a respectful, professional way.
