Our Mission

We were created in early 2025 to connect and unite all Azerbaijani students across all campuses of Shanghai Jiao Tong University. Our goal is to build a welcoming community where students can support one another, share practical guidance, and feel at home in Shanghai. Through events and cultural representation, we also aim to share Azerbaijani culture with the wider SJTU community in a respectful, professional way.
