SJTU 130th Anniversary

Featured April 6, 2025

Representing Azerbaijan 🇦🇿 at SJTU's Biggest Celebration

On April 6th, SJTU.AZ had the honour of representing the Republic of Azerbaijan at Shanghai Jiao Tong University's 130th Anniversary — the largest and most celebrated annual event on campus. Our booth became a warm meeting point for students, faculty, and guests from across the university and beyond.

Visitors were welcomed with a curated spread of traditional Azerbaijani pastries, homemade jams, and authentic drinks — a genuine taste of the Caucasus in the heart of Shanghai. As a token of our culture and hospitality, guests were gifted beautifully presented journals and books to explore the Azerbaijani language and dive deeper into our history and traditions.

The event was a wonderful reminder of why we do what we do: building bridges between cultures, sharing what makes Azerbaijan unique, and strengthening our community here in Shanghai. A heartfelt thank you to everyone who stopped by, engaged with us, and made the day truly memorable.
